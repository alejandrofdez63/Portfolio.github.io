Chicken Roast Pot

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 1
Baby Potatoes 6
Garlic head 1
Cup of chicken stock 1

List of steps:

  1. Prepare your chicken. (Season it with condiments).
  2. Chop your vegetables into tiny pieces.
  3. Sear the chicken.
  4. Stuff your chicken with the vegetables and pour chicken broth.
  5. Cover the chicken and roast it in the oven.

Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 5 people
Ingredients Quantity
Onion 1
Diced tomatoes 2 cans
Heavy cream A cup
Fresh basil Half a cup
Olive Oil A tablespoon

List of steps:

  1. Heat up the olive oil and chop the onion.
  2. Pour in the tomatoes and mix them with the onion.
  3. Add the vegetables to simmer.
  4. Blend the soup.
  5. Add basil and the cream.
"

Lemon Bars

  • Time: 50 minutes
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 2 sticks
Sugar Half a cup
Flour 2 cups
Lemon juice Half a cup
Eggs 4

List of steps:

  1. Prepare the crust by mixing the ingredients together.
  2. Bake the crust in the oven.
  3. Prepare the lemon filling.
  4. Pour the filling over the crust.
  5. Bake the bars.
"
